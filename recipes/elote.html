Corn Elote

Ingredients:

  • Shucked Corn
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cojita Cheese
  • Caynee Powder or Tajin
  • Cilantro

Directions:

  1. Grill corn until cooked
  2. Coat, or roll, corn in mayo
  3. Roll corn in cojita cheese
  4. Top with spices
  5. Breifly heat corn on grill (use tin foil) and serve
