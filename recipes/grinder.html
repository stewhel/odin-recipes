Italian Grinder Sandwhich

Ingredients:

  • Italian Hero Roll
  • Ham
  • Salami
  • Pepperoni
  • Provolone
  • 1/2 White Onion
  • 1 Tomatoe
  • 1/4 Cup Banana Peppers
  • 1/2 Head Iceberg Lettuce
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 Tablespoons Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1/4 Cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 Tablespoon Dijon/Stone Ground Mustard
  • 1 Tablespoon Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Tablespoon Oregano

Directions:

  1. Thinly shred two cups of iceberg lettuce and add to a large bowl
  2. Thinly cop banana peppers and add to bowl
  3. Combine mayonnaise, red wine vinegar, choped garlic, mustard, and seasonings to bowl
  4. Thinly slice onion and tomatoes
  5. Layer the meats, onion, and tomatoes on roll
  6. Add the grinder mixture on top
  7. Serve with chips and Dr. Pepper
