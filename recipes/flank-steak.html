Flank Steak w/ Post Marinde

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds flank steak
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 Cup Mirin
  • 2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon Sriracha
  • 1 Tablespoon White Vinegar
  • 2 Teaspoons Sesame Oil
  • 1 Bunch Scallions

Directions:

  • Dry steak with a paper towel or cloth
  • Coat steak with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper
  • Mix soy sauce, brown sugar, sriracha, vineage, and sesame oil to create marinde
  • Grill steak and scallions to preference
  • Remove steak and let rest for 10 minutes
  • Cut steak and top with marinade
