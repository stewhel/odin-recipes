Pork Sambal Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 1lb Ground Pork
  • 1 box Thin Spaghetti Noodles
  • 2" Ginger
  • 8 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 2 Sprigs Basil
  • 1/3 Cup Sambal (Hot Chili Paste)
  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 Cup Rice Vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • 2 Tablespoons Tomatoe Paste
  • Salt to Taste
  • Drizzle Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Directions:

  1. Bring salted water to boil and cook Spaghetti
  2. In seperate pan, saute minced garlic and ginger with EVOO
  3. Add half of pork to saute pan, cook until well-browned
  4. Add rest of the pork and sugar, and mix thoroughly
  5. Add tomatoe paste and basil spring, stir until darkened
  6. Add Sambal, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and 2 cups of water
  7. Bring to a simmer and reduce heat
  8. Cook until sauce is thickened, about 20-30 mins
  9. Combine sauce, butter, and spaghetti noodles
  10. Garnish with extra basil
