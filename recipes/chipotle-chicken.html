Chipotle Chicken

This is a recipe to make a knock-off version of Chiptole's chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 2lb Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • 2 Tablesppons Adobe Sauce (use Goya's Chioptle Peppers in Adobe Sauce)
  • 1/2 Large Red Onion
  • 2 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 Tablespoons Chili Powder
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 2 Teaspoons Oregano
  • 2 Teaspoons Cumin

Directions:

  1. In a blender, mix together the half onion, adobe sauce, olive oil, and seasonings
  2. Cover the chicken in the marinade
  3. Refridgerate and let rest for at least two hours
  4. Cook chicken on grill on medium-low heat
  5. Dice chicken in to cubes
