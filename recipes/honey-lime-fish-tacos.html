Honey-Lime Fish Tacos w/ Mango Salsa

Taco Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Fish
  • Corn Tortilles
  • 1 Tablespoon Honey
  • 1 Teaspoon Cumin
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Lime

Salsa Ingredients:

  • 1 Mango
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1/4 Red Onion
  • Small Bunch Cilantro

Preperation:

  1. Cook the fish, either on a pan or with an air fryer
  2. Finely chop and mix the salsa ingredients, and add the juice of one lime
  3. Mix togehter the honey, cumin, salt, and juice of one lime
  4. After the fish has cooked, add the honey-lime mixture
  5. Toast the tortillas and serve
