Chicken Parm w/ Homemade Sauce

Sauce Ingredients:

  • 28oz Canned Tomato Purree
  • 1/4 Cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 8-10 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Tablespoon Tomato Paste
  • 1 Teaspoon Red Pepper Flake
  • 1 Teaspoon Oregano
  • 1 Teaspoon Sugar
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Chicken Ingredients:

  • 4 Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Lemon
  • 5 Garlic Cloves
  • 1/4 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tablespoon Onion Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  • 4 Cups Panko
  • 2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 12 ounces Mozzarella
  • 8 ounces Grated Parmesan
  • Sprig Basil

Directions:

  1. Heat EVOO in a heavy pan or dutch oven
  2. Add diced onion and garlic, sautee
  3. Add tomato paste, stir together until darkened (about 5 minutes)
  4. Add canned tomato puree and sauce seasonings, including sugar
  5. Let simmer for 30 minutes
  6. While sauce is cooking, prepare chicken for frying
  7. Filet chicken breasts to 1/2" thick and flatten with a rolling pin (or wine bottle!)
  8. Squeeze lemon juice over chicken and top with finely chopped garlic
  9. Let rest 30 minutes
  10. In three seperate bowls, prepare flour and seasoning, beaten eggs, and panko
  11. Dip chicken in flour then coat with egg wash, before finishing in panko
  12. Heat vegetable oil in pan
  13. Fry chicken
  14. Remove chicken and top with sauce then sliced mozzzarella and grated parmesan
  15. Broil for 5 minutes unitl cheese is melted and slightly browned
  16. Remove and garnish with basil
