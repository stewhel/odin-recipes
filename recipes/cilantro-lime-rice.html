Cilantro Lime Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 Tablespoon Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2 Bunch Cilantro
  • 2 Limes
  • Optional: Black or Red Beans

Directions:

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear (4+ times)
  2. Add rice and 2 cups water to InstantPot (1:1 ratio) and cook
  3. Fluff rice and add vegetable oil (this prevents clumping)
  4. Add juice of two limes
  5. Thinly cop cilantro and add to rice
  6. Mix in beans if desired
